    User showcase¶

    Since its creation for OUI.sncf in 2016, Tock has been used by more and more teams to design and build conversational bots dedicated to various use cases :

    • business to customer and business to business
    • e-commerce, transactions, payment
    • assistance, care, help desks
    • FAQ and decision trees

    This page presents various assistants and products built with Tock.

    TOSIT association¶

    The Tock solution is currently is the early stages to join the TOSIT (The Open Source I Trust), an association dedicated to support Open Source and Free Software.

    Founded by Carrefour, EDF, Enedis, Orange, Pôle Emploi and SNCF, TOSIT now gathers other important members, such as the (French) Ministère des Armées, Société Générale or MAIF for instance.

    To know more about TOSIT, please visit http://tosit.fr/
